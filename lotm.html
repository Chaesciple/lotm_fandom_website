The Lord Of The Mysteries (诡秘之主)

"The Lord of the Mysteries" is a gripping fantasy-mystery tale centered around Klein Moretti, a former journalist embarking on a journey through a world shrouded in secrecy and danger. As he unravels the mysteries of the city of Tingen and beyond, he confronts ancient prophecies, hidden organizations, and his own inner conflicts. With each step, Klein navigates a treacherous path, forming alliances, confronting adversaries, and seeking understanding amidst the enigmatic forces that shape his world. Through its intricate world-building and compelling narrative, the story captivates readers with themes of power, ambition, and the pursuit of knowledge, inviting them on an unforgettable adventure filled with intrigue and peril.

Author: Cuttlefish That Loves Diving

Novel Volumes
Volume 1 - Clown
The first volume contains 213 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 1 to 213. It details the story of Klein Moretti as he first arrives in the world of Lord of the Mysteries, his becoming a Beyonder, and his adventures as a Nighthawk of Tingen City. It covers events from June 28th to September 16th in the year 1349
Volume 2 - Faceless
The second volume contains 269 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 214 to 482. It focuses on Klein in the identity of a private detective, Sherlock Moriarty, in the city of Backlund. It covers events from September 16th, 1349 to the new year of 1350.
Volume 3 - Traveller
The third volume contains 250 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 483 to 732. It tells the story of Klein Moretti as he leaves Backlund and travels at sea under the identity of the crazy adventurer Gehrman Sparrow.
Volume 4 - Undying
The fourth volume contains 214 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 733 to 946. It depicts Klein's story in the identity of Dwayne Dantès as he attempts to enter the nobles' circle of the Loen Kingdom and his revenge on Ince Zangwill.
Volume 5 - Red Priest
The fifth volume contains 204 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 947 to 1150. It portrays of Klein's investigation of Great Smog of Backlund and the beginning of the world war at the end of 1350.
Volume 6 - Lightseeker
The sixth volume contains 116 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 1151 to 1266. It tells the story of Klein's journey in the Forsaken Land of the Gods.
Volume 7 - The Hanged Man
The seventh volume contains 87 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 1267 to 1353.
Volume 8 - The Fool
The eighth and final volume contains 41 chapters in total, consisting of chapters 1354 to 1394.

Pathways to Divine

A Pathway (途径, Tújìng), or Path of the Divine (神之途径, Shén zhī tújìng), corresponds to a series of 10 named potion formulas that give access to supernatural and mystical powers. Once a being has drunk a potion, they become a Beyonder of that pathway. Each pathway belongs to a group containing one or more neighboring pathways, which a Beyonder can safely switch within at High-Sequence. At the end of each pathway, only one Beyonder can attain divinity and become the True God of that pathway.

Pre-epoch

In a time more ancient than the Modern Era, the sleeping Original Creator had naturally split into multiple personas. "They" were also in a state of slumber, but "They" began to use the authority and Beyonder Characteristics "They" controlled to influence the world, making many preparations for the true dissociation when the Original Creator awakened. Among the multiple personas, The Primordial One and The Celestial Worthy of Heaven and Earth were the most powerful and active Great Old Ones on Earth. Many folklores and legends in this time were based on actual mysterious events.
